Hot Chocolate

Hot chocolate without all the guilt.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ¾ cups coconut water
  • 3 ounces dark chocolate bar with almonds, broken into pieces
  • 1 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 32 miniature marshmallows

Steps:

  1. Stir coconut water and dark chocolate together in a saucepan over medium heat until chocolate melts.
  2. Sift cocoa powder into the chocolate mixture, stirring constantly, until mixture simmers, about 5 minutes.
  3. Pour into mugs and top with marshmallows.
