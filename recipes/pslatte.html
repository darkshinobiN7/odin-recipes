Pumpkin Spice Latte

An attempt to reproduce the famous drink from Starbucks!

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups hot whole milk
  • 4 teaspoons white sugar
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ teaspoon pumpkin pie spice
  • 6 ounces double-strength brewed coffee
  • 3 tablespoons sweetened whipped cream
  • 3 pinches pumpkin pie spice

Steps:

  1. Combine the hot milk, sugar, vanilla extract, and pumpkin pie spice in a blender.
  2. Blend until frothy.
  3. Pour the mixture into 3 coffee mugs to about 2/3 full.
  4. Pour 2 ounces coffee into each mug.
  5. Garnish each mug with whipped topping and pumpkin pie spice.
