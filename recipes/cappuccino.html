Cappuccino Mix

Here is a homemade dry cappuccino mix in a jar. Make it for yourself, or give it as a gift.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup powdered non-dairy creamer
  • 1 cup instant chocolate drink mix
  • ¾ cup instant coffee granules
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ¼ teaspoon ground nutmeg

Steps:

  1. In a medium sized glass jar, combine the non-dairy creamer, chocolate drink mix, instant coffee, sugar, cinnamon and nutmeg.
  2. Mix well.
  3. To prepare, place 2 tablespoons mix in a mug, pour in 3/4 cup boiling water, and stir until dissolved.
  4. Serve hot.
